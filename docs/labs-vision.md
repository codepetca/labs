# Codepet Labs Vision

Codepet Labs is an independent, startup-style builder lab for invited students.
It is supported and funded by Codepet.ca, and is not affiliated with,
sponsored by, or operated by any school.

The lab helps students learn modern software development by building small,
useful projects with AI tools, GitHub, and weekly review.

## Why

Pika is a classroom management system like Google Classroom. Students used Pika
this past semester and helped give feedback. Labs gives that classroom
perspective a practical summer outlet.

Students are already exploring AI tools. Labs gives them a supervised way to
use those tools to build, test, explain, and improve real work.

## What

Codepet Labs is a summer builder group. It is currently by invitation. It
operates more like a small builder incubator than a school program.

Students work on Codepet and Pika-adjacent prototypes, docs, demos, QA, product
ideas, and small tools.

It is not a formal class, job, internship, or path to unsupervised production
access.

## How

The work is highly self-directed. Students choose or are assigned a small
project, make progress independently, and bring updates to a weekly check-in.

- Weekly 1-2 hour check-in
- About 10 hours per week over the summer
- GitHub-based progress
- AI-assisted learning and coding
- Demos, feedback, and iteration

Projects use mock data first. Labs work does not use real student data or
unsupervised production Pika access.

## What Students Get

Students get practical experience with AI-assisted software development,
GitHub, product thinking, debugging, testing, UI, docs, demos, and technical
communication.

They also get portfolio-worthy work, clearer attribution, and a better sense of
what real software work feels like.

## What Codepet Gets

Codepet gets fresh student perspective from people who have used Pika in real
classrooms.

Labs can produce useful prototypes, sharper product ideas, better feedback
loops, and future features worth exploring. It also helps Codepet understand how
students learn and build with AI when they have structure, trust, and clear
boundaries.
